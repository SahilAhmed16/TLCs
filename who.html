Lighting Consultancy & Planning

We have all grown up surrounded by very simple and limited sources of light namely tube light, incandescent bulbs & CFL lights. With the growing demand for more efficient and aesthetic sources of light along with the concurrent growth of LED technology, lighting has become a very complex and sophisticated subject.
To simplify lighting as a subject, we strongly think that the user and designer must have a full grasp and observation of illumination, taking into account its different parameters and specifications. This is where wea are the beacon for your project.

Hospitality

Hospitality lighting creates a warm and inviting atmosphere for guests. It blends functionality and ambiance, ensuring comfort while enhancing the overall experience of the space.

Residential

Residential lighting provides both functionality and ambiance within homes. It combines task, accent, and decorative lighting to enhance comfort, highlight architectural features, and create a cozy, personalized atmosphere.

Commercial

Commercial lighting focuses on illuminating business spaces such as offices, retail stores, and warehouses. It aims to improve productivity, safety, and the customer experience, while balancing energy efficiency and visual comfort.

Underwater

Underwater lighting is designed for submerged environments, providing visibility while showcasing the beauty of aquatic life and structures. Itâ€™s often used for pools, fountains, or marine environments, creating dramatic effects and ensuring safety in aquatic settings.

Landscape

Landscape lighting enhances the outdoor environment by illuminating gardens, walkways, and architectural features. It adds beauty, depth, and security while creating a warm, inviting atmosphere at night.

Industrial

Industrial lighting provides bright, durable illumination for environments such as factories and warehouses. It prioritizes visibility, safety, and efficiency, with a focus on energy-saving solutions and long-lasting performance in demanding settings.

Bespoke Signage

Bespoke signage lighting is custom-designed to highlight unique signage. It ensures maximum visibility, enhances brand presence, and creates a striking visual impact, tailored to the specific needs and aesthetics of the sign.
